What does our application do?

DECIDE is a comprehensive application revolutionizing vote management, offering streamlined processes across key sections like Census, Voting, Groups, Users, Votes, and Questions. With an intuitive interface, robust security, and detailed reporting, DECIDE empowers organizations to enhance transparency and democratic participation.

Census

The Census section facilitates efficient management of voter registration. Administrators can handle voter lists, verify eligibility, and maintain an accurate database for electoral processes.

Voting

The Voting section simplifies the creation, management, and monitoring of voting processes. It provides intuitive tools to configure elections, define candidates, and ensure transparent tracking throughout the voting process.

Groups

The Groups section allows the creation and management of voter groups, facilitating internal elections and the assignment of specific permissions.

Users

The Users section provides a comprehensive panel for user management. From account creation to role assignment and permissions, DECIDE simplifies the administration of user participation in the system.

Votes

The Votes section offers detailed tracking of votes, ensuring the integrity of the voting process. DECIDE provides a dedicated space to monitor and analyze election results, facilitating informed decision-making.

Questions

The Questions section encourages user interaction and participation. Users can submit and vote on questions, fostering transparency and open communication within the community.
